ABOUT ME

Let's bring your idea to life

Web Design - Web Administration - Logo & Brand Design - Advertisement Design - Javascript Widget Design

I fell in love with computers at an early age and started learning to program BASIC before I was a teenager. By 16, I was coding webpages for family, friends, and a few clients. After high school, I joined the Navy and had to put my coding on hold. What I thought was going to be a brief stop in the military turned into a 21-year-career, taking me all over the world and even back to my hometown in New Hampshire as a recruiter for a bit. Along the way, I would still help friends with webpages, logos, and even took a few paid gigs. After retiring from the Navy, I fell back in love with coding full-time and started taking on clients to help bring their businesses, projects, and ideas to life.

Chris Gorham


Welcome to my website. My goal is to showcase my work and have you get to know me, what I'm all about, and what I can do for your business, project, or idea. I have my formal resume linked up top and if you scroll below (or click the portfolio link at the top), you'll see some of the projects I've worked on. I also have some website demos and templates set up to give you some ideas of what your website could look like.

Outside of coding, I'm a husband and a father of two, residing on Maryland's Eastern Shore. In my spare time I enjoy going for walks with my kids, dabbling in cryptocurrency / NFTs, and geeking out over my favorite bands, books, and movies (check out my Top 10 page!)
